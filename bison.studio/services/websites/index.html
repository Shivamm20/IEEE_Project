At Bison Studio, we pride ourselves in building cutting-edge websites that feature a high level of finesse through subtle animation, keeping users engaged, but without compromising on functionality. We are consummate thinkers and jump into each project brain-first to help clients meet their objectives and get their business recognised within the online marketplace.

Our studio focuses on design & development of websites, apps and other digital products with an emphasis on cutting-edge user experiences and clean, efficient code. From slick, forward-looking websites to mobile-optimised pages and interactive user-focused experiences, our web design & web development experts make it happen.

Our team of expert web developers & web designers work in cahoose as we truly believe that digital products and services are best made by designers and engineers working together in the same room. Our clients are part of this journey as they get the opportunity to see our progress along the way. Our studio loves to grace on interesting problems. We want to co-create the right digital strategies for your business, because no one client is ever the same. Show us a complicated project and we’ll show you a logical path to smashing through every obstacle with a well-balanced mix between brains and brawn.

explore
the process

1

Digital Strategy

Firstly, we start with an exploratory workshop to understand your organization, your goals, your customers, their expectations and behavior, and how your website factors into it all. We work closely with our clients in order to come up with a strategy document that covers, user personas, storyboards, feature lists & findings, and wireframes

2

Design Phase

Once armed with all information, we’ll set to work designing the user interface for the entire website and both mobile and desktop. You will be able to see how the pages and designs fit together and experience the website before the building starts. We also creating prototypes in order for us to rapidly test and optimise the website flow before beginning development

3

Testing & deployment

Once the designs are approved, we’ll build the website based on the approach agreed upon at the start of the project. Our in-house development team takes an agile approach to ensure you see tangible progress throughout the project. Our websites are built to last — and you’ll find it easy to manage content and updates in the back-end.
